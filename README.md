# Student-College-App

Welcome to KVNM, the Unofficial college application for Kantha Vibhag Navnirman Mandal, Surat. This app is designed to make your college experience more convenient and efficient. With KVNM, you have access to a wealth of information and resources, including a faculty database, notices section, e-book section, and a convenient CGPA calculator. The app also boasts an intuitive and user-friendly interface, making it easy to navigate and access the features you need. Whether you're a current student, prospective student, or simply someone interested in the college, KVNM is the perfect tool to stay connected and informed.

## Features

- 👨‍🏫 Faculty Database : Now, you can search amongst faculties and get their data within seconds. 

- 🔔 Notices Section : No need to open Gmail! Get all the notices on your fingertips. 

- 📖 E-books : Stuck in finding pdf? No worries, we've got you covered. Access all the learning material on KVNM

- ➗ CGPA Calculator : Got your marks, but where do you stand? Know your GPA / CGPA by entering your marks and course credits.
 
- Offline Capabilities : You dont have to wait until the data gets loaded everytime you open the app. KVNM comes with offline capabilities where users just need to load the data once and view it even if there is no internet access.
